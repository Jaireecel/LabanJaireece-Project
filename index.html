about us

Better words. Better beans. Better you.

Jai's Book & Kafe is a perfect combination between café and library that's not only offers the best brewed coffee or espresso but also provides home-like, cozy, and comfortable environment surrounded by plenty of books that will soon establish in Olongapo City. It has an aim to start the business to fulfill the needs of coffee and literature lovers, and provide them with a welcoming environment to congregate, interact and unwind.
